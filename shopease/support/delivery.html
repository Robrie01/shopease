Delivery

Everything you need to know about shipping and tracking your ShopEase order.

Where is my order? ›

Once your order has been dispatched you will receive a confirmation email with your tracking number. You can use this to track your parcel on the courier's website.

Orders are typically dispatched within 1 working day of being placed. If you have not received a dispatch email after 2 working days please contact our support team.

What delivery options are available? ›

We offer three delivery options at checkout:

  • Standard Delivery — 3 to 5 working days — £3.99
  • Express Delivery — Next working day (order before 2pm) — £7.99
  • Free Delivery — 5 to 7 working days — Free

All orders are delivered to UK addresses only.

How long will my delivery take? ›

Delivery timeframes depend on the option selected at checkout. Working days are Monday to Friday, excluding UK bank holidays.

If your order has not arrived within the expected timeframe please allow one additional working day before contacting us, as occasional courier delays can occur.

Do you deliver outside the UK? ›

Currently ShopEase only delivers to addresses within the United Kingdom. We hope to offer international delivery in the future.

What happens if I miss my delivery? ›

If you are not available when the courier attempts delivery, they will typically leave a card with instructions to rearrange delivery or collect from a local depot. You can also use the courier's tracking system to manage your delivery preferences.

My tracking shows delivered but I have not received my order ›

Please check with neighbours and any safe places around your property. If you still cannot locate your parcel, contact our support team with your order number and we will investigate with the courier on your behalf.
