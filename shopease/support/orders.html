Order Issues

Help with cancellations, incorrect items and missing parcels.

Can I cancel or amend my order? ›

You may be able to cancel or amend your order if it has not yet been dispatched. Please contact our support team as soon as possible with your order number.

Once an order has been dispatched it cannot be cancelled. In this case you would need to return the item once received.

Something is wrong with my item ›

If your item arrives damaged or faulty, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery. Include your order number and a photo of the issue where possible.

We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including return postage.

I have a missing item from my order ›

If an item is missing from your delivery, please check your dispatch confirmation email as some orders may be sent in multiple parcels.

If the item is not listed as a separate shipment, contact our support team with your order number and we will investigate and resolve this for you.

I received the wrong item ›

We apologise for this. Please contact our support team with your order number and a description or photo of the item received. We will arrange collection of the incorrect item and dispatch the correct one at no extra cost.

My order says processing but I have not received any update ›

Orders are usually dispatched within 1 working day. If your order has been showing as processing for more than 2 working days please contact our support team and we will look into this for you.
