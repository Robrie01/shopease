Returns & Refunds

Information about returning items and receiving your refund.

What is your returns policy? ›

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return any unused item in its original condition and packaging. This is in line with the Consumer Rights Act 2015.

Items that are damaged, used or not in their original packaging may not be eligible for a full refund.

How do I return an item? ›

To return an item:

  • Contact our support team with your order number and the item you wish to return
  • We will provide you with a return label and instructions
  • Package the item securely in its original packaging
  • Drop it off at your nearest post office or courier collection point

Once we receive and inspect the item your refund will be processed within 5 working days.

How long does a refund take? ›

Refunds are processed within 5 working days of us receiving your returned item. The time it takes to appear in your account depends on your bank or card provider, but is typically 3 to 5 additional working days.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it? ›

We do not currently offer direct exchanges. To get a different item, please return the original for a refund and place a new order for the item you want.

What items cannot be returned? ›

The following items are non-returnable:

  • Items that have been used or opened
  • Items not in their original packaging
  • Perishable goods
  • Digital products or gift vouchers once redeemed
Who pays for return postage? ›

If the item is faulty or we sent the wrong item, ShopEase will cover the return postage cost. For change-of-mind returns, the cost of return postage is the responsibility of the customer.
