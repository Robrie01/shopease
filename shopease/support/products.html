Product & Stock

Help with product availability, information and saving items.

Is a product in stock? ›

Stock availability is shown on each product card in the catalogue. Items marked as Out of Stock are currently unavailable. Items showing Only X left are low on stock and may sell out soon.

Stock levels update in real time as purchases are made.

Will an out-of-stock item come back? ›

We restock popular items regularly. Adding an out-of-stock item to your wishlist is a great way to keep track of it — check back regularly to see if it has become available again.

How do I save an item for later? ›

Click the heart icon on any product card to add it to your wishlist. You can view and manage your wishlist from the Wishlist page in the sidebar. Items in your wishlist are saved to your account.

Where can I find more information about a product? ›

Product details including category, price and stock are shown on each product card. If you need further information about a specific product, please contact our support team and we will be happy to help.

Are prices shown inclusive of VAT? ›

Yes. All prices on ShopEase are displayed in GBP and include 20% VAT. A breakdown of VAT is shown at checkout before you complete your order.
