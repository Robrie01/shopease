Technical

Help with your account, password and site issues.

I cannot log in to my account ›

Please check that you are using the correct email address and password. Passwords are case-sensitive.

If you have forgotten your password, contact our support team and we will help you reset it.

How do I reset my password? ›

You can change your password from the Settings page once logged in. Go to Settings in the sidebar and find the Change Password section. You will need to enter your current password to set a new one.

If you cannot log in to access Settings, contact our support team for assistance.

How do I update my account details? ›

Account settings including your name, email preferences and password can be managed from the Settings page, accessible from the sidebar when logged in.

How do I delete my account? ›

You can request account deletion from the Settings page under the Danger Zone section. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data in line with UK GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure). Account data is deleted within 30 days of the request.

The site is not loading correctly ›

Try the following steps:

  • Refresh the page with Ctrl + Shift + R (or Cmd + Shift + R on Mac) to clear cached files
  • Try a different browser
  • Clear your browser cookies and cache
  • Disable any browser extensions that may be interfering

If the issue persists please contact our support team with details of the browser and device you are using.

How do I manage my cookie preferences? ›

Your cookie preferences can be managed via the cookie banner shown on your first visit. You can choose Essential Only or Accept All. To change your preference, clear your browser cookies and reload the site to see the banner again.
